5307. A mass is a relatively large number of people with a common interest, though they may not be in close proximity (Lofland 1993), such as players of the popular Facebook game Farmville.         A public, on the other hand, is an unorganized, relatively diffused group of people who share ideas, such as the Libertarian political party.       While these two types of crowds are similar, they are not the same.
